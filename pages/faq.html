General Questions

What services does Centuries Mutual offer?

We offer comprehensive financial services including tax preparation, insurance solutions, and wealth management services tailored to individual and business needs.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling (888) 324-6642 or using our online booking system. Our team will match you with the appropriate expert based on your needs.

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM CST. We also offer virtual consultations for your convenience.

Tax Services

What tax preparation services do you offer?

We offer individual and business tax preparation, tax planning, IRS representation, and audit support services.

What documents do I need for tax preparation?

Required documents typically include W-2s, 1099s, receipts for deductions, and previous year's tax returns. We'll provide a detailed checklist based on your situation.

Do you offer year-round tax planning?

Yes, we provide year-round tax planning services to help you minimize tax liability and make informed financial decisions.

Insurance Services

What types of insurance do you offer?

We offer health insurance, life insurance, disability insurance, and group insurance plans for businesses.

How do I choose the right insurance plan?

Our insurance experts will assess your needs and help you compare different plans to find the best coverage for your situation.

Can I change my insurance plan?

Yes, you can modify your coverage during open enrollment periods or after qualifying life events.

Client Portal

How do I access the client portal?

You can access the client portal through our website using your email and password. First-time users will need to register for an account.

What can I do in the client portal?

Through the portal, you can view documents, schedule appointments, communicate with your advisor, and access important forms.
